My range burner caps keep rusting. Is that normal here?

Richmond's proximity to the park raises indoor humidity above average. Cast-iron caps corrode faster. We can replace them and recommend drying caps after cleaning to slow re-rusting.

My dishwasher smells even after cleaning. What's wrong?
