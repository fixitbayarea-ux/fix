Thermador Appliance Repair

Thermador invented the residential wall oven, introduced the first cooktop with a downdraft ventilation system, and designed the Star Burner — a five-pointed, 56-port burner that distributes flame more evenly than any conventional round burner. Their appliances are engineering statements, and every Thermador product reflects that ambition: CrystalDry dishwashers use volcanic zeolite minerals to absorb moisture, Freedom refrigerator columns feature SoftClose hinges built to residential furniture standards, and their steam ovens include precision-calibrated boilers. This is not commodity appliance design.

The complexity that makes Thermador exceptional also makes it unforgiving of generic repair attempts. Star Burner flame ports clog differently than standard burners and require individual cleaning of all 56 openings. CrystalDry zeolite cartridges are consumable components that most owners do not realize need periodic replacement. Steam oven boilers require descaling with specific solutions at specific intervals. Common Problems We Fix

  • Thermador Star Burner produces uneven flame — individual flame ports are clogged with grease and food residue, disrupting the star pattern
  • Thermador dishwasher AutoAir door does not open after the cycle — actuator arm has disconnected or the door-push motor has failed
  • Thermador Freedom refrigerator column running warm — condenser fan motor has failed inside the built-in compartment with restricted airflow
  • Thermador steam oven descale alert won't clear after running the cycle — mineral buildup has physically blocked the steam generator passages
  • Thermador wall oven not reaching set temperature — hidden rear bake element has open-circuited at one of its terminal connections
  • Thermador induction cooktop displaying E error — cracked ceramic glass surface is disrupting the magnetic sensor communication underneath
  • Thermador range ExtraLow simmer flame keeps extinguishing — gas pressure regulator was set too low during original installation
  • Thermador CrystalDry no longer effective — zeolite mineral cartridge is exhausted after 3 to 5 years of regular dishwasher use

What to Check Before You Call

  • Clean Thermador Star Burner caps with warm soapy water and use a toothpick or pin to carefully clear each individual flame port
  • If the Thermador dishwasher door does not auto-open after the cycle, check the settings menu to confirm AutoAir is enabled
  • For Thermador oven temperature issues, run the built-in calibration test — most models allow offset adjustments in the settings menu
  • Check that your Thermador range has proper gas supply pressure — underpowered lines cause the simmer flame to blow out
  • Note the model and serial number from inside the door or the rear panel — Thermador part numbers are model-specific

FAQ

My Thermador Star Burner flame is uneven and patchy. What is wrong?

The Star Burner has 56 individual flame ports arranged in a five-pointed star pattern. When ports clog with cooking grease or food residue, the flame becomes uneven. We deep-clean all 56 ports and verify each burner produces a uniform flame pattern before leaving.

What is CrystalDry and when does it need replacing?

CrystalDry uses a natural mineral called zeolite that absorbs moisture during the drying cycle and converts it to heat. After 3 to 5 years of regular use, the mineral loses its absorptive capacity. We replace the cartridge and test drying performance to confirm the fix.

Is Thermador repair more expensive than standard brands?

Yes, typically 20 to 40 percent more due to higher OEM part costs. However, Thermador appliances cost $5,000 to $15,000 to replace, so repair remains the significantly better financial decision in most cases.

Can you fix Thermador Freedom refrigerator columns?

Yes. Freedom columns are a regular part of our service work. We handle compressor diagnostics, SoftClose hinge adjustments, and panel realignment that is often needed after kitchen renovations disturb the surrounding cabinetry.

My Thermador steam oven keeps showing a descale alert. How is that fixed?

We flush the steam generator with a commercial descaling solution, clear the mineral deposits from the internal passages, and reset the descale counter in the control board. If the generator is permanently scaled shut, we replace the unit.
