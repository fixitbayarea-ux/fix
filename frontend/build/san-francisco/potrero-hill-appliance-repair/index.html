Quick Answers

Does the Potrero Hill sun really kill fridges faster?

It contributes. A south-facing kitchen that hits 85°F forces the compressor to run 40% more than a 70°F kitchen. Ensuring 2 inches of clearance around the unit and cleaning the condenser annually makes a measurable difference.

My new condo dishwasher won't drain. It was just installed.

Common in new construction. Installers sometimes forget to remove the knockout plug from the drain inlet port, or the drain hose has no high loop. We check both — usually a 15-minute fix.

Can you fix appliances in a kitchen island?

Yes. Island-installed cooktops, dishwashers, and wine coolers require pulling from below or above. We've done it in dozens of Potrero Hill condos — we bring appliance sliders and protect your flooring.
