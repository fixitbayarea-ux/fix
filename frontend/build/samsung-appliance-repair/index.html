Common Problems We Fix

  • Samsung refrigerator ice maker frozen solid — defrost cycle fails to reach the ice maker compartment, a known multi-generation design issue
  • Samsung washer UE error on every load — suspension rods worn thin after 4 to 6 years, causing the drum to slam during spin
  • Samsung dryer runs but clothes stay damp — heating element open-circuited or thermal fuse blown from restricted exhaust vent
  • Samsung Smart Hub screen frozen or black — software hang requiring a board-level reset, not a hardware replacement
  • Samsung dishwasher LC leak code — moisture detected on the base sensor, typically from a loose inlet hose clamp or cracked sump gasket
  • Samsung range igniter glows red but gas never lights — igniter drawing below the minimum amperage needed to open the gas safety valve
  • Samsung washer door locked after cycle ends — door lock actuator stuck in the engaged position, requiring manual release and replacement
  • Samsung refrigerator clicking every few seconds — compressor start relay failing intermittently, causing repeated start attempts

What to Check Before You Call

  • For a frozen Samsung ice maker, try the manual defrost mode — hold Fridge and Energy Saver buttons simultaneously for 8 seconds
  • Clear Samsung washer error codes by running a Spin Only cycle after removing excess laundry from the drum
  • Check your Samsung dryer lint filter and exhaust vent — a restricted vent is the most common cause of no-heat conditions
  • If the Smart Hub screen is frozen, unplug the refrigerator for 2 minutes to force a software reboot
  • Write down any error code from the display before calling — Samsung codes start with letters like UE, LC, SE, or numbers like 5E

FAQ

Can you fix the Samsung ice maker problem everyone talks about?

Yes. Samsung's ice maker freeze-up is the single most common Samsung defect we repair. We replace the failed defrost heater, recalibrate the defrost timer, and when applicable install Samsung's updated ice maker assembly that addresses the original design flaw.

My Samsung Smart Hub screen is black. Does the whole fridge need replacing?

Almost never. Smart Hub failures are typically a software freeze or a loose ribbon cable behind the screen. We perform a board-level diagnostic and reset or replace the display module only — not the refrigerator.

How long do Samsung appliances typically last?

Samsung refrigerators average 10 to 14 years, washers 8 to 12 years, and dishwashers 7 to 10 years with proper maintenance. If your unit is under 8 years old, repair is usually more cost-effective than replacement.

Samsung washer vibrates across the floor during spin. Is that normal?

No. Samsung front-loaders develop excessive vibration when the suspension rods wear out, typically after 4 to 6 years. We replace all four rods as a set because replacing one at a time leads to repeat failures. Parts and labor usually run $220 to $350.

My Samsung dryer runs but clothes are still damp after a full cycle.

The three most likely causes are a failed heating element, a blown thermal fuse, or a clogged exhaust vent. We test all three during the diagnostic and repair whatever is causing the no-heat condition.
